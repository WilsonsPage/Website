Why Cardio Matters

Cardiovascular training improves heart health, endurance, mental clarity, and supports fat loss. It's a key pillar of a complete fitness lifestyle.

Types of Cardio

  • Walking: Low impact, easy to start, great for beginners.
  • Running: Builds stamina, burns calories, boosts mood.
  • Cycling & Rowing: Great for joint-friendly, full-body cardio.
  • HIIT: High-intensity intervals for fat burn and time efficiency.

Beginner’s Weekly Plan

  • 3x brisk walking (30 minutes)
  • 2x light jog or intervals (20 minutes)
  • 1x recovery walk or low-intensity cardio

Tips for Progress

  • Track time, distance, and heart rate.
  • Focus on consistent breathing patterns.
  • Warm up and cool down properly.
  • Invest in supportive footwear.

Cardio & Recovery

Don’t overtrain — cardio should energize you, not drain you. Hydrate, stretch, and prioritize recovery days.
