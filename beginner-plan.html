12-Week Beginner Running Plan

Designed to help you ease into running with a progressive approach

Plan Overview

This 12-week plan is flexible, allowing you to choose your rest days and adjust the running days based on your schedule. You can always walk if the effort exceeds the recommended level in the plan, which helps keep your heart rate manageable. If you feel like doing more, we encourage walking on extra days instead of running to help your body adjust and prevent injury. Listen to your body and remember: rest is just as important as running!
